Join Our Loyalty Program

At Saencrystal Specialty Stores, we value our customers! Our Loyalty Program is designed to reward you for your continued patronage. Earn points, enjoy exclusive discounts, and get more value with every purchase.

Benefits of Joining

  • Earn Reward Points: Get points for every Naira spent in our store and online. The more you shop, the more points you accumulate.
  • Redeem Points for Discounts: Use your accumulated points to get discounts on future purchases. Save money while you shop!
  • Exclusive Member Offers: Receive special promotions, early access to sales, and members-only deals.
  • Birthday Rewards: Get a special treat or discount during your birthday month as a thank you for being a loyal customer.
  • Personalized Experience: Enjoy recommendations and offers tailored to your shopping preferences.

How to Sign Up

Joining our Loyalty Program is easy and free!

  • In-Store: Ask any of our cashiers or customer service representatives to sign you up during your next visit. All we need is your name and phone number.
